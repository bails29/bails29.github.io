Statistical attribution of changes in historical streamflow

In my DPhil (PhD) research, I am using statistical methods to understand the effects of environmental change on streamflow and water availability across many catchments simultaneously. These large-sample approaches facilitate interpretation of why hydrologic behavior differs across catchments in response to human-caused environmental changes like land cover and climate change. I have a particular interest in afforestation, deforestation, and changes in wetland area, which may have direct hydrological effects, as well as influencing streamflow via land-atmosphere feedbacks.
